AFK Water and Shimmer Dupe

    • Tags

    Wiring

    Glitches

    Mobs

    This build uses the Liquid Dupe glitch (Method 5).

    AFK Liquid duper works with both water and shimmer but not with lava and honey. It also persists world reloads.

    Post-mech version

    This mechanism consists of 2 liquid sensors (use (Water) or (Any) for water and (Any) for shimmer), a logic gate lamp, a logic gate (AND, OR or XOR), a grate and 2 connected pumps.
    The top liquid sensor is connected to a logic gate lamp. Its purpose is to send a signal to "refresh" the duper. The logic gate with a lamp serves as a kind of inverter so that this "refresh" happens only when there's no water. The sensor in the pool is connected to the grate. This is a way to limit how much water you want in the pool and stop the flow when it's reached.

    To use it, simply pour a bucket of water over the top block. It may start refreshing sporadically, but it will stabilize after a while.

    With shimmer, add another pump that goes from shimmer to the top pump and trigger it once.
    Alternatively, build this contraption right under aether, dig a hole up to the pool, let some flow through and then close the gap.

    Pre-hardmode version

    Realistically, this setup can be created pre-boss, as placed wires can be found naturally, and active/inactive stone blocks and explosives can be shimmered to obtain wires as an item for crafting pumps and timers.

    This version works similarly to the previous one. It's being "refreshed" on a 5-second timer, which makes it much slower, as it wastes time on unneeded "refreshes".

    Notes

    This glitch is very inconsistent and not much is known about it. These contraptions make it more reliable, but it's still not quite perfect. When it does break, pouring more liquid on the top block normally fixes it.

    The dimensions of these builds are not that strict. The shown contraptions are made to be as compact as they can get.
    The splitting block doesn't have to be a singular block, it can be a long row. It can also be moved higher.
    The top basin can't be made smaller as it will output too little water before stopping to dupe. However, it can be made larger. Although, if it's too wide, the water will likely disappear.
    The hole must always be 1 block wide, can't change this.
    The pool's size and position relative to the duper can be changed freely. Just make sure the water can actually go into the pool :)
