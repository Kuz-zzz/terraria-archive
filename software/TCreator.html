    Modding

    TCreator is a tool inspired by MCreator, specifically designed for creating custom elements in the game Terraria. With TCreator, you can easily design custom items, and tiles, with more planned for your Terraria mods.

    At the moment this tool is still in early stages of development and is not very user-friendly.
