    Miscellanious

    Demize.gg is a website made by demize with multiple tools and guides mostly aimed at beginners.

    Terraria Maker: Terraria character designer. The result can be downloaded in PNG format.

    Random Class: a tool to randomly choose a Terraria class. Also supports throwing/rogue, healer and bard classes from mods.

    Drop Calculator: calculates the chance of getting a drop after defeating a certain number of enemies.

    Shimmer Finder: calculator that uses the name of the first Guide to determine the location of Shimmer.

    Small Dungeon?: a simple answer to a very common question.

    Terraria Glossary: explains what AIW, NWNC, AIW and boss abbreviations mean.
