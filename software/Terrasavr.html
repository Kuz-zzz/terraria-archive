Terrasavr

    • Developer YellowAfterlife
    Status Up-to-Date
    Platform Browser
    Tags

    Player Editor

    Terrasavr is a web-based inventory and profile editor. It can also be downloaded for offline use.

    In March 2023 a preview of a rewrite of Terrasavr, Terrasavr+ was released. It loads faster and has more mobile-friendly UI.

    tAPIbara was an experimental version of Terrasavr, modified to work with tAPI player files, launched in January 2015. Unfortunately, since June 2015 the website no longer works due to lack of demand for it.
