TConvert

    • Developer trigger_segfault
    Status Up-to-Date
    Platform Windows
    Tags

    Modding

    Reverse Engineering

    Resource Packs

    TConvert is a combination tool for managing Terraria content resources. Extract from Xnbs, convert to Xnbs, backup, and restore. The unofficial sequel to TExtract.
    Supports: Images, Sounds, Sprite Fonts, ReLogic's Dynamic Sprite Fonts, and Wave Banks.

    TConvert is a successor to TExtract.
