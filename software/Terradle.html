Terradle

    • Developer cxhuy
    Status Up-to-Date
    Platform Browser
    Tags

    Game

    Terradle is a wordle-like game inspired by the game Terraria.

    • The objective of this game is to guess the WOTD (weapon of the day) based on the stats and hints provided throughout the game.
    • For every weapon submitted, each stat will appear as green, yellow, or red depending on how close it is to the stats of the WOTD.
    • Green means the stat is identical to that of the WOTD.
    • Yellow (only for obtained) means the guessed weapon shares a way of being obtained with the WOTD, indicating that there's an overlap.
    • Red means that the stat is wrong. For each stat, there will be arrows indicating whether the guessed weapon's stat needs to be higher or lower to match the WOTD's corresponding stat. ex) Damage: 54↑ means that the damage stat of the WOTD is higher than 54.
