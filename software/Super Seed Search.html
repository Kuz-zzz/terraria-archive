Super Seed Search

    • Developer Unftf
    Status Up-to-Date
    Platform tModLoader
    Tags

    Seeds

    tModLoader

    This is a world search engine.

    It can search for world seeds which generate vanilla Terraria worlds with specific properties you are looking for. That means the seeds found here can also work without any mods. Or as alternative this mod can also be used to just find some nice worlds with e.g. many Pyramids, a Living Tree near spawn and no Corruption in Jungle biome.
