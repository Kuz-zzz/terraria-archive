Cart Dash

    • Mode Any
    Status Works
    Platform Any
    Tags

    Movement

    Minecart

    Cart dash or chicken teriyaki chilli paprika bread toasted tomato onion jalapeno sweet chilli sweet onion hot chilli american peppered american cheese is a trick you can use to instantly accelerate to the maximum speed on a minecart by mounting a track mid-dash on certain rail formations.

    To efficiently perform the cart dash, you need a featherfall potion and any equipped minecart (except Digging Molecart). You may need to rebind the quick mount key as it's not very easy to press R as you're dashing.

    Upwards Teriyaki

    This is the most useful application of the cart dash. Simply dash into this rail formation and press the quick mount button, and you can fly all the way from spawn to the dungeon in small worlds (assuming you have a featherfall buff and a minecart equipped).

    You can also use it for travelling across larger worlds using an ice rod. With this method, if you're going left, use quick mount and if right, smart cursor to mount the rails.

    Horizontal Cart Dash

    Rarely used outside PvP on pre-made arenas and some obscure challenges.

    There are 2 rail formations that allow you to cart dash. Both can be performed on the ground and in the air.

    When you teriyaki the "wrong" way with the diagonal formation, you need to quick mount only when you are just slightly past the rails. Otherwise, you will just get stuck.

    Lore

    The term "chicken teriyaki chilli paprika bread toasted tomato onion jalapeno sweet chilli sweet onion hot chilli american peppered american cheese" originated in the Terraria Speedruners! Discord server when they were trying to come up with a name for the "cart yeet". Sbubby was the one who suggested this obscure name and everyone agreed on using it.
